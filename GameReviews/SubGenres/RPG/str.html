Strategy Subgenres

4X games are strategy games where players control a faction or empire, focusing on exploration, expansion, resource management, and military conquest. These games are typically set in large, open worlds with complex economic and diplomatic systems.

4X (Explore, Expand, Exploit, Exterminate)

Grand strategy games emphasize large-scale, long-term planning, where players manage entire nations or empires, controlling aspects like diplomacy, warfare, economics, and politics. These games often span centuries or decades, allowing for deep, strategic decision-making.

Grand Strategy Wargame

In MOBA games, two teams of players control individual heroes with unique abilities, working together to destroy the opposing team's base while defending their own. Gameplay involves strategic teamwork, lane control, and resource management.

Multiplayer Online Battle Arena

RTS games require players to manage resources, build units, and command armies in real time, focusing on both tactical combat and strategic resource allocation.

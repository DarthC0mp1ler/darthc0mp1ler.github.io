Action-Adventure Subgenres

Action-Adventure: This subgenre combines the fast-paced combat of action games with exploration and puzzle-solving elements. Players often engage in combat while navigating intricate environments and completing story-driven missions.

Action-Adventure

Hack & Slash: Focused on melee combat, Hack & Slash games emphasize rapid, combo-based attacks against waves of enemies. Players use swords, axes, or other weapons to execute flashy moves and defeat hordes of opponents.

Hack & Slash

Survival: In survival games, players must manage limited resources while avoiding threats in a hostile environment. They often involve crafting, building, and staying alive against enemies, natural hazards, or hunger.

Survival

Survival Horror: Combining the tense atmosphere of horror with survival mechanics, these games challenge players to navigate terrifying settings while managing scarce resources. Titles like Resident Evil and Silent Hill use suspense, limited ammunition, and psychological elements to create a sense of fear and urgency.

Survival & Horror

Stealth: Stealth games prioritize avoiding detection, encouraging players to use tactics like sneaking, hiding, and silent takedowns. Success often requires patience and strategy rather than direct combat.

Stealth
